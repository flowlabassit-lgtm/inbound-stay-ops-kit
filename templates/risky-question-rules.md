# Risky Question Rules

Route these to host review or block them.

## Block

- Refunds
- Cancellations
- Payment changes
- Platform policy
- Off-platform booking or payment
- Emergency instructions that replace local emergency services
- Legal, tax, or insurance questions
- Review manipulation

## Host Review

- Early check-in
- Late check-out
- Luggage storage
- Lost items
- Damage claims
- Noise complaints
- Exact access details
- Unexpected extra guests
- Parking exceptions

## Safe Auto-Reply

- Standard check-in time
- Standard check-out time
- Public house rules
- Public transport overview
- Nearby convenience store
- Non-sensitive amenity guidance
